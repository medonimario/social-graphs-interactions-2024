{{Short description|German physician and materialist philosopher (1819–1873)}}
'''Heinrich Adam Friedrich Czolbe''' (30 December 1819, [[Katzke]] bei [[Danzig]] (now, a village of [[Kaczki]]; [[:pl:Kaczki (województwo pomorskie)|pl]]) - 19 February 1873, [[Königsberg]]) was a [[physician]] and [[Materialism|materialist]] [[philosopher]] from the [[Kingdom of Prussia]].

== Literary works ==
* ''Neue Darstellung des [[Sensualism]]us'', 1855
* ''Entstehung des Selbstbewußtseins. Eine Antwort an Herrn Professor Lotze'', Leipzig 1856 ([https://dlib.stanford.edu:6521/text1/dd-ill/czolbe.pdf PDF]{{Dead link|date=January 2020 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }})
* ''Die Grenzen und der Ursprung des menschlichen Erkentniss'', 1865

== Further reading ==
