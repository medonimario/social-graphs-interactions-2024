'''Alejandro Octavio Deustua Escarza''' (22 March 1849 – 6 August 1945)<ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=kNQaAAAAMAAJ&q=Alejandro+Octavio+Deustua+Escarza Profile of Alejandro Octavio Deustua Escarza]</ref> was a [[Peru]]vian [[philosopher]], [[Teacher|educator]] and [[politician|statesman]]. He was the [[Prime Minister of Peru]] from 9 August 1902 until 4 November 1902.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://rulers.org/rulp1.html#peru |title=Countries Pa-Pe |publisher=Rulers.org |access-date=2012-07-22}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://gw.geneanet.org/fracarbo?lang=en&p=alejandro+octavio&n=deustua+escarza|title = Family tree of Alejandro Octavio Deustua Escarza}}</ref>

==Biography==
Deustua was born in [[Huancayo]], Peru. His parents were Remigio Deustua and Toribia Escarza. Deustua studied in Guadalupe National School and graduated from [[National University of San Marcos|Universidad de San Marcos]] in [[Lima]], Peru. He died on 6 August 1945 at the age of 96 in [[Lima]], [[Peru]].
