'''Hans-Georg Backhaus''' (born 1929) is a German [[Marxian economist]] and philosopher. He is considered one of the most important theorists on the field of [[Karl Marx|Marx's]] [[Labor theory of value#Marx.27s contribution|theory of value]]. He began a long-term cooperation with [[Helmut Reichelt]] already from his years of university studies.
