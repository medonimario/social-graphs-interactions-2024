'''José Pablo Feinmann''' (29 March 1943 – 17 December 2021) was an Argentine philosopher, writer, [[playwright]], and television host.<ref>[http://www.mertin-litag.de/authors_htm/Feinmann-J.htm José Pablo Feinmann]</ref> He also penned several screenplays for domestic film production and international coproductions.

Born to Abraham and Elena (de Albuquerque) Feinmann,<ref>[https://www.pagina12.com.ar/diario/contratapa/13-117422-2008-12-28.html Las Navidades de Bongo]</ref> Feinmann was a [[Peronist Youth]] militant during the 1970s, considering [[Peronism]] as a real mass movement with the potential to change the country for the better. Nevertheless, he opposed armed violence to achieve political ends, criticizing the [[foco]] theory of [[Che Guevara]] which, years after the [[Cuban Revolution]], became popular in some sectors of the Marxist-Peronist movement, such as [[Montoneros]]. 

Feinmann abandoned Peronism in the 1990s, during the [[neoliberal]] government of [[Carlos Menem]]. He later became a supporter of [[Left-wing politics|left-wing]] Peronist president [[Cristina Fernandez]].

==Death==
Feinmann died from complications of a stroke on 17 December 2021, at the age of 78.<ref>{{cite news |title=Murió José Pablo Feinmann, escritor y filósofo de lectura imprescindible |url=https://www.pagina12.com.ar/390085-murio-jose-pablo-feinmann-escritor-y-filosofo-de-lectura-imp |access-date=18 December 2021 |publisher=Página 12 |date=17 December 2021}}</ref>
