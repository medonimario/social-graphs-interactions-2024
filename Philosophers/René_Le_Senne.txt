'''René Le Senne''' (born '''Ernest René Lesenne'''; 8 July 1882, in [[Elbeuf]] – 1 October 1954, in [[Neuilly-sur-Seine]]) was a [[French idealism|French idealist]] philosopher and psychologist.
<ref name="DictPR">{{Cite book|title=Dictionary of philosophy and religion: Eastern and Western thought|last=Reese|first=William|date=1996|publisher=Humanities Press|isbn=0391038648|location=Atlantic Highlands, N.J.|oclc=33983842|page=406}}</ref>

* ''Le devoir'', Paris, Alcan, 1930
* ''Traité de caractérologie'', Paris, Puf, 1945

* ''Le mensonge et le caractère'', Paris, F. Alcan, 1930
