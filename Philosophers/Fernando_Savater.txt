'''Fernando Fernández-Savater Martín''' (born 21 June 1947 at Basque city of San Sebastián) is a Spanish [[philosopher]], [[essayist]] and author.

== Early years and career ==
Born in [[San Sebastián]], he was an [[Ethics]] professor at the [[University of the Basque Country]] for over a decade. Presently he is a [[Philosophy]] professor at the [[Complutense University of Madrid]]. He has won several accolades for his literary work, which covers issues as diverse as [[contemporary ethics]], politics, cinema and literary studies. In 1990, Savater and columnist and publisher, [[Javier Pradera]], founded the magazine, ''Claves de Razón Práctica''.<ref>{{cite news |first=José Andrés |last=Rojo |title=The watchman of Spain's transition signs off |url=https://elpais.com/elpais/2011/11/21/inenglish/1321856446_850210.html |newspaper=[[El País]] |date=21 November 2011 |access-date=23 November 2011 |language=es |location=Madrid}}</ref>

In November 2012 he was awarded the prestigious [[Octavio Paz]] Prize of Poetry and Essay.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://elpais.com/cultura/2012/11/16/actualidad/1353093382_007641.html |title=Fernando Savater, premio de Poesía y Ensayo Octavio Paz |last=Constenla |first=Tereixa |date=16 November 2012 |access-date=17 November 2012 |location=Madrid |language=es |newspaper=[[El País]] |publisher=Prisa}}</ref>

In that same year 2012, with issue number 222 (May–June 2012), he became Editor in Chief of "Claves de razón práctica" ([[Spanish language|Spanish]] for "Keys for Practical Reason"), a critical thought and cultural crusading review founded by [[Javier Pradera]] in 1990, thus giving start to a second era of this prestigious publication, with an editorial board composed of Basilio Baltasar, [[Francisco Calvo Serraller]], Joaquín Estefanía, Carlos García Gual, [[Santos Juliá]] and José María Ridao.<ref>ARCE (Asociación de Revistas Culturales de España) http://www.revistasculturales.com/revistas/15/claves-de-razon-practica/ {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190522093054/http://www.revistasculturales.com/revistas/15/claves-de-razon-practica/ |date=2019-05-22 }}</ref>

== Political activism ==
Savater founded a political party currently disbanded called ''Unión, progreso y democracia'' (Spanish for [[Union, Progress and Democracy]] and often shortened as UPyD) with former socialists MP [[Rosa Díez]], [[Albert Boadella]] and [[Mario Vargas Llosa]] in order to oppose different nationalist movements in Spanish mainstream politics.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.elespanol.com/espana/20160918/156484861_0.html |title=Fernando Savater: "Soy de UPyD, pero Ciudadanos es la mejor opción de las que se presentan" |date=19 September 2016 |access-date=13 August 2018 |last=Ramírez |first=Daniel |language=es |newspaper=El Español}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=https://elpais.com/politica/2016/06/14/actualidad/1465922642_677899.html |title=UPyD se pone las gafas de Savater |last=Mateo |first=Juan José |date=15 June 2016 |access-date=13 August 2018 |location=Madrid |language=es |newspaper=[[El País]] |publisher=Prisa}}</ref>

Savater was one of the intellectuals and politicians who endorsed the 2012 manifesto of the "reconversion.es" platform vouching for a recentralization of the country, and considered a precursor of the far-right [[Vox (political party)|Vox]].<ref>{{Cite journal|url=https://www.politicaprosa.com/catalunya-i-la-espana-viva-de-vox/|journal=Política & Prosa|author-link=Xavier Casals|first=Xavier|last=Casals|title=Catalunya i 'la España viva' de Vox|date=1 April 2019}}</ref> In 2018, Savater adhered to the manifesto ''Manifiesto por la historia y la libertad'' promoted by [[Francisco Franco National Foundation]], decrying the [[Law of Historical Memory]].<ref>{{Cite journal|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190925005102/https://www.noticiasdegipuzkoa.eus/2018/09/01/politica/san-gil-mayor-oreja-y-gallardon-apoyan-un-manifiesto-de-la-fundacion-franco|url=https://www.noticiasdegipuzkoa.eus/2018/09/01/politica/san-gil-mayor-oreja-y-gallardon-apoyan-un-manifiesto-de-la-fundacion-franco|title=San Gil, Mayor Oreja y Gallardón apoyan un manifiesto de la Fundación Franco|journal=Noticias de Gipuzkoa|date=1 September 2018|first=Carlos C.|last=Borra|archive-date=2019-09-25}}</ref>

He has taken an active part in several organizations engaged with peace in the Basque Country and against [[ETA (separatist group)|terrorism]] and [[Basque nationalism]], such as ''Movimiento por la Paz y la No Violencia'', ''Gesto por la Paz'', ''[[Foro Ermua]]'', and currently ''[[¡Basta Ya!]]''

== Personal life ==
Savater is also known as a philosophy [[popular science|popularizer]], TV host, lecturer and for his political [[activism]]. 

He defines himself as an [[agnostic]],<ref>{{cite web |url=http://bloc.mabosch.info/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/4.1.4.2%20%20ESC%C3%89PTICOS,%20AGN%C3%93STICOS,%20ATEOS.pdf |title=Escepticismo, agnosticismo y ateísmo Concepto de Dios, panteísmo, deismo |date=November 2012 |access-date=13 August 2018 |language=es |first=Miquel Àngel |last=Bosch i Fridrin |work=Mabosch}}</ref> an anglophile and a defender of the [[Age of Enlightenment|Enlightenment]] in the [[Voltaire]] tradition.<ref>{{cite journal |url=http://www.apostadigital.com/revistav3/hemeroteca/oneca3.pdf |title=Voltaire o el caos de las ideas claras |last=Oneca Agurruza |first=Iñaki |date=11 October 2004 |access-date=13 August 2018 |issn=1696-7348 |journal=Aposta. Revista de Ciencias Sociales |language=es }}</ref>
