'''Karl-Otto Apel''' ({{IPA|de|ˈaːpl̩|lang}}; 15 March 1922 – 15 May 2017) was a [[German people|German]] [[philosopher]] and Professor Emeritus at the [[University of Frankfurt am Main]]. He specialized on the [[philosophy of language]] and was thus considered a [[communication theorist]]. He developed a distinctive philosophical approach which he coined ''[[transcendental pragmatics]].''

==Life==
Apel grew up during the political crises of the [[Weimar Republic]]. In 1940, he was a war volunteer with his entire graduating class. After the [[Second World War]], Apel studied from 1945 to 1950 at the [[University of Bonn]], first history and [[intellectual history]], before he committed himself as a student of [[Erich Rothacker]] on [[philosophy]].<ref>{{cite book|last1=Hösle|first1=Vittorio|title=Die Krise der Gegenwart und die Verantwortung der Philosophie|date=1997|publisher=C.H.Beck|isbn=978-3406392740|pages=110–115|language=de}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|last1=Reese-Schäfer|first1=Walter|title=Karl-Otto Apel zur Einführung|date=1990|publisher=Junius Verlag|isbn=978-3885068617|location=Hamburg|pages=15–21|language=de}}</ref> In 1950, he received his doctorate from Bonn with a thesis on [[Martin Heidegger]].

==Academic positions==
Apel was appointed lecturer at the [[University of Mainz]] in 1961. He was a full professor of philosophy at the [[University of Kiel]] from 1962 to 1969, at the [[University of Saarbrücken]] from 1969 to 1972, and at the [[University of Frankfurt am Main]] from 1972 to 1990.<ref>{{cite web|last1=|title=Apel, Karl-Otto|url=http://d-nb.info/gnd/118503596/about/html|accessdate=19 August 2014|website=d-nb.info|publisher=Deutsche National Biblothek}}</ref> In 1990, he transferred to [[emeritus]] status. He has held a number of visiting and guest professorships at universities around the world.

He was made a Member of the [[Academia Europaea]] in 1989 and a Full Member of the [[Academia Scientiarum et Artium Europaea]] in 1993. In 2001, he was awarded the [[Order of Merit of the Federal Republic of Germany]].

==Philosophical work<!--'Transcendental pragmatics' redirects here-->==
Apel worked in [[ethics]], the [[philosophy of language]] and [[human sciences]]. He wrote extensively in these fields, publishing mostly in German. Apel's work brings together the [[analytical philosophy|analytical]] and [[continental philosophy|Continental]] philosophical traditions, especially [[pragmatism]] and the [[critical theory]] of the [[Frankfurt School]]. He developed a distinctive philosophical approach which he called '''transcendental pragmatics'''<!--boldface per WP:R#PLA--> (''Transzendentalpragmatik'').<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://www.worldcat.org/oclc/29495586|title=Transzendentalpragmatik: ein Symposion für Karl-Otto Apel|date=1993|publisher=Suhrkamp|others=Andreas Dorschel|isbn=3-518-28681-1|edition=1. Aufl|location=Frankfurt am Main|language=de|oclc=29495586}}</ref>

In ''Understanding and Explanation: A Transcendental-Pragmatic Perspective'', Apel reformulated the difference between understanding (''[[Verstehen]]'') and explanation (''Erklärung''), which originated in the [[hermeneutics]] of [[Wilhelm Dilthey]] and [[interpretive sociology]] of [[Max Weber]], on the basis of a [[Charles Sanders Peirce|Peircean]]-inspired transcendental-pragmatic account of language. This account of the "[[lifeworld]]" would become an element of the theory of communicative action<ref>{{Cite book|last=Witzany|first=Günther|url=https://www.worldcat.org/oclc/27105465|title=Transzendentalpragmatik und Ek-sistenz: Normenbegründung, Normendurchsetzung|date=1991|publisher=Verlag Die Blaue Eule|isbn=3-89206-317-6|location=Essen|oclc=27105465}}</ref> and [[discourse ethics]], which Apel co-developed with [[Jürgen Habermas]]. Strategic rationality both claim to stand in need of communicative rationality that is seen as, in several regards, more fundamental.<ref>{{Cite journal|last=Dorschel|first=Andreas|date=1989|title=Ist strategisches Handeln ergänzungsbedürftig? Karl-Otto Apels These und ihre Begründung|url=https://www.cambridge.org/core/journals/european-journal-of-sociology-archives-europeennes-de-sociologie/article/abs/ist-strategisches-handeln-erganzungsbedurftig-karlotto-apels-these-und-ihre-begrundung/42F3211AF704BDE294075B39AC24596D|journal=European Journal of Sociology / Archives Européennes de Sociologie|language=en|volume=30|issue=1|pages=123–149|doi=10.1017/S0003975600005816|s2cid=143643321 |issn=1474-0583}}</ref> While sympathetic to Habermas's ''Theory of Communicative Action'', Apel has been critical of aspects of Habermas's approach. Apel has proposed that a theory of communication should be grounded in the transcendental-pragmatic conditions of communication. After taking his point of departure from Apel, Habermas has moved towards a "weak [[Transcendental philosophy|transcendentalism]]" that is more closely tied to empirical social inquiry.

Apel also wrote works on [[Charles Sanders Peirce]] and is a past president of the C. S. Peirce Society.

An early German-speaking adversary of so-called [[critical rationalism]], Apel published a critique of the philosophy of [[Karl Popper]]: In ''Transformation der Philosophie'' (1973), Apel charged Popper with being guilty of, amongst other things, a [[Pragmatics|pragmatic]] contradiction.<ref>{{Cite book|last1=Clement|first1=Elizabeth|last2=Demonque|first2=Chantal|last3=Hansen-Love|first3=Laurence|last4=Kahn|first4=Pierre|title=La philosophie de A à Z|publisher=Hatier|year=1994|location=Paris|pages=19–20|chapter=Apel, Karl-Otto}}</ref><ref>{{cite book |title=Towards a Transformation of Philosophy |series=Marquette Studies in Philosophy |volume=20 |first=Karl-Otto |last=Apel |translator-first1=Glyn |translator-last1=Adey |translator-first2=David |translator-last2=Fisby |location=Milwaukee |year=1998 |publisher=Marquette University Press |isbn=0-87462-619-6 }}</ref>

From the 1970s to the 1990s, Apel influenced other philosophers writing in [[Europe]], the [[Americas]], and [[Asia]]. Apel died on 15 May 2017 at the age of 95.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2017-05-16|title=Philosoph: Karl-Otto Apel ist tot|url=https://www.zeit.de/zustimmung?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.zeit.de%2Fkultur%2F2017-05%2Fphilosoph-karl-otto-apel-tot|access-date=2021-04-03|website=www.zeit.de|language=de}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|date=2017-05-16|title=Philosoph Karl-Otto Apel gestorben|url=https://science.orf.at/v2/stories/2843480/|access-date=2021-04-03|website=science.ORF.at|language=de}}</ref>
