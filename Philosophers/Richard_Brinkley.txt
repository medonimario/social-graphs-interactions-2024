'''Richard Brinkley'''<ref>Richardus Brinkley, Richard Brinkel, Brinkelius</ref> (died c.1379) was an [[English people|English]] [[Franciscan]] [[scholastic philosopher]] and theologian. He was at the [[University of Oxford]] in the mid-fourteenth century; he produced a ''Summa Logicae'' in a [[nominalist]] vein in the 1360s or early 1370s, and other works.
